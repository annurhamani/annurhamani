TAPAH DISTRICT COUNCIL

During my internship at the Tapah District Council, I gained practical experience that enriched my knowledge in information management. I worked closely with the Corporate, Public Relations, and Tourism Unit, where I learned about handling public complaints through the SISPAA system.

This involved registering, updating, and monitoring complaints to ensure efficient service delivery to the community. Additionally, I observed the administrative operations of a local government office, which gave me valuable insight into public relations, data management, and organizational procedures.
